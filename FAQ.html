Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some common questions and answers

  • What is carbon offsetting?

    Carbon offsetting is the act of compensating for carbon dioxide emissions by investing in projects that reduce or remove an equivalent amount of greenhouse gases from the atmosphere.

  • How does the carbon surfing calculator work?

    The carbon surfing calculator estimates your carbon footprint associated with surfing activities and suggests a donation amount to offset that footprint. The donated amount goes towards environmental projects aimed at reducing carbon emissions.

  • Can I contribute more than the suggested donation?

    Yes, you can contribute more if you wish. Every additional contribution helps support environmental initiatives further.
